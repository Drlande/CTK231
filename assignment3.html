Welcome to the Creative Technologies Program

The interdisciplinary Creative Technologies major emphasizes design and practice in the integration of digital technologies and the fine arts. Students gain experience in video, sound, electronic music, gaming, motion graphics, AR/VR, UI/UX, web, and programming.

Featured 200-Level Class

201 - Basic Skills In The Digital Arts (3)
Introduction to the history, theories, current trends, practices, and core media in the digital arts.

Program Details

Students must maintain a GPA of 2.50 to continue in sequences such as Audio and Music Production, Game Design, or Interdisciplinary Technologies.
