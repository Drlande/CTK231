Retention Requirements

  • Sequence in Audio and Music Production and Interdisciplinary Technologies – Students will be allowed to continue in this sequence if they meet and maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50.
  • Sequence in Game Design – Students will be allowed to continue in this sequence if they meet and maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50 and a cumulative GPA of 3.0 within the major.

Standards for Progress in the Major

Students will be allowed to continue in the Creative Technologies program if they maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and have demonstrated competency through an intermediate portfolio review. This ensures students are prepared for advanced courses.
