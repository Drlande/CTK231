Program Requirements

Students in the Creative Technologies program must complete core classes, maintain a minimum GPA, and choose from specialized sequences like Game Design, Audio and Music Production, or Interdisciplinary Technologies. The program combines theory with hands-on practice to prepare students for careers in dynamic creative industries.

Core 200-Level Classes

201 - Basic Skills In The Digital Arts (3)
Introduction to the history, theories, current trends, and core media in the digital arts.

202 - Foundations: Audio Theory (3)
Fundamentals of audio theory applied to the physics and aesthetics of sound.

203 - Foundations: Recording and Sampling (3)
Technical skills required for recording and sampling instruments and the human voice.
