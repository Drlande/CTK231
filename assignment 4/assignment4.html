The interdisciplinary Creative Technologies major emphasizes integration of digital technologies with the arts.

Featured 200-Level Class

201 - Basic Skills In The Digital Arts (3)

Program Overview

Students must maintain a GPA of 2.50 to continue in sequences such as Audio and Music Production, Game Design, or Interdisciplinary Technologies. This program helps students develop skills across a variety of creative and technical fields while exploring new possibilities in digital innovation. With guidance from expert faculty and hands-on projects, graduates are prepared to succeed in industries that demand both artistic vision and technological expertise.
